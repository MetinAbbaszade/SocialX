SocialX Community

Rules and Terms of Use

Welcome to the SocialX community. To ensure a positive experience for all users, please familiarize yourself with our rules and terms of use. These guidelines help maintain our community as a respectful, informative, and engaging platform for everyone.

Community Rules

General Behavior

  • Be respectful to all community members. Disagreements are fine, personal attacks are not.
  • Contribute to discussions in a meaningful way that adds value to the conversation.
  • Before posting, check if similar content already exists to prevent duplicate discussions.
  • Give proper attribution when sharing others' work or ideas.

Communication Standards

  • Use clear and concise language to help others understand your point.
  • Stay on topic within threads and discussions.
  • Avoid ALL CAPS as it is interpreted as shouting in online communication.
  • Be patient with new members who may not be familiar with community norms.

Prohibited Content

The following content types are strictly prohibited and may result in content removal and/or account suspension:

Unacceptable Content

  • Hate Speech: Content that promotes discrimination, hostility, or violence against individuals based on race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or similar attributes.
  • Harassment: Content targeting individuals for abuse, threats, or intimidation.
  • Misinformation: Deliberately false or misleading information that could cause harm.
  • NSFW Content: Explicit sexual, violent, or disturbing content without appropriate warnings and context.
  • Spam: Excessive self-promotion, irrelevant content, or repetitive posting.
  • Illegal Activity: Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities including but not limited to drug trafficking, theft, fraud, or copyright infringement.

Moderation Policy

Enforcement Actions

Our moderators enforce community rules using a graduated system:

  1. Warnings: First-time or minor violations typically result in a warning.
  2. Content Removal: Posts or comments that violate our rules will be removed.
  3. Temporary Suspension: Repeated violations may result in temporary account suspension.
  4. Permanent Ban: Severe or persistent rule violations may lead to permanent removal from the community.

Reporting Process

If you encounter content that violates our rules, please report it by:

  1. Using the "Report" button next to the content
  2. Selecting the appropriate reason for the report
  3. Providing additional context when prompted

Reports are anonymous and reviewed by our moderation team promptly.

Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error, you may appeal the decision by contacting our moderation team at moderation@socialx.com.
